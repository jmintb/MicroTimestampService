Timestamp microservice

This is as microservice created for the FreeCodeCamp TimeStamp micoservice challenge. Pass a time in one of the formats shown below, and you will get a json response with the time as a unix timstamp and in human readable form. Enjoy!

Usage:

Huma readable format: https://mysterious-beach-99402.herokuapp.com/December%2015,%202015
Unix timestamp: https://mysterious-beach-99402.herokuapp.com/1450137600

Output:

{"unix":1450137600,"natural":"Tue Dec 15 2015"}
